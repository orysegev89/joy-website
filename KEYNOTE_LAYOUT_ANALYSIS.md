- Positioned at top of content area

- **Alignment**: Centered

- **Font size**: Medium, readable (appears to be around 14-16px)

- **Paragraphs**: 4 distinct paragraphs with clear spacing between them

## Portfolio Grid Section
- **Layout**: 2x2 grid (4 images total)
- **Spacing**: Small gaps between images (appears to be 8-16px)
- **Image aspect ratio**: Landscape/cinematic (approximately 16:9 or similar)
- **Width**: Full width of content container
- **Positioning**: Below bio text with spacing

## Overall Spacing
- Top padding from navigation: Generous (60-80px)
- Between name and bio: Medium (40px)
- Between bio and portfolio: Medium (60px)
- Side margins: Contained within max-width container

## Typography
- **Director name**: Very large, bold, uppercase, tracking wide
- **Bio text**: Clean sans-serif, white, medium weight
- **Line spacing**: 1.6-1.8 line height for readability

## Key Design Principles
1. **Minimalist**: Black background, white text, no decorative elements
2. **Centered**: All content centered horizontally
3. **Hierarchy**: Clear visual hierarchy (name → bio → portfolio)
4. **Breathing room**: Generous spacing throughout
5. **Professional**: Clean, sophisticated aesthetic
